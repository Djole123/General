The Doctors

Background Information:

Over many years, people have been giving arguments as to who was the best Doctor. The answer is simple... As you can see from the chart below, their characters, although having few similarities are widely different. Each Doctor played their part to the same complex degree as the variable personalities of many normal humans. This inadvertently means that each Doctor would be suited to a different person. To answer the question many have been asking; All the Doctors were the best, but to each his own in terms of preference. My personal favourite was the way David Tennant depicted this alien hero. Many of his catch phrases are simply great, and his choice of style is overly appealing to me.
